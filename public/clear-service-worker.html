Clear Service Worker & Cache

Ready to clear service workers and caches...

If automatic clearing doesn't work:

  1. Open Chrome DevTools: F12 or Right-click → Inspect
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. In the left sidebar, click Service Workers
  4. Click Unregister for each worker listed
  5. In the left sidebar, click Storage
  6. Click the Clear site data button
  7. Do a hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)

Alternative method:

  1. Go to Chrome Settings: chrome://settings/content/all
  2. Search for localhost:3000
  3. Click the trash icon to delete all site data
